Who is Cécile & Why is She Contacting Us?

I'm a student in search of an apprenticeship

Telling stories has always been my passion. I studied film, but kept gravitating towards sketching and painting.

Today I’ve realized that my calling is in the digital realm of visual storytelling. After this epipfany I've embarked on a new education in Digital Media at Roskilde Technical School.

In order to furfill my new education I'll need an apprenticeship.

I don’t regret getting my master’s in Film and Media studies; there I've learned invaluable lessons on visual communication and narratives.

Now I hope to use and develop this knowlegde during an apprenticship in an awesome company such as yours!

Quick facts about Cécile

  • She has both a BA and MA in Film- and Media Studies from Copenhagen University.
  • She has a Danish/Swiss dual citizenship and is fluent in Danish, English and French.
  • She has experience producing film, text, photos, drawings & music - as well as a combination of all the latter.
  • She's always aiming to expand her knowlegde and experiment with new ways of communicating.
  • She's studying Digital Media and now needs an apprenticeship in designing and programming apps and websites.
  • Her given name is Cécile Anna-Camille Zimmermann but she also goes by Cille.
